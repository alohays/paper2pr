## Source Details

### Stat 1 & 2: PwC Global AI Jobs Barometer (56% wage premium + 66% faster skills)

- **Report:** "Fearless Future: PwC's 2025 Global AI Jobs Barometer"
- **Publisher:** PwC Global
- **Publication date:** June 2025
- **Primary URL:** https://www.pwc.com/gx/en/news-room/press-releases/2025/ai-linked-to-a-fourfold-increase-in-productivity-growth.html
- **Full report PDF:** https://www.pwc.com/gx/en/issues/artificial-intelligence/job-barometer/2025/report.pdf
- **Methodology:** Analysis of close to one billion job ads from six continents; data through end of 2024
- **Exact quotes available:**
  - "Jobs which require AI skills offer a wage premium (over similar roles that don't require AI skills) in every industry analysed, with the average premium hitting **56%**, up from 25% last year."
  - "The skills sought by employers are changing **66% faster** in occupations most exposed to AI, up from 25% last year."
- **Recommended inline citation:** (PwC Global AI Jobs Barometer, 2025)

### Stat 3: Gartner AI Agent Enterprise Prediction (40% by EOY 2026)

- **Report:** Gartner press release — "Gartner Predicts 40% of Enterprise Apps Will Feature Task-Specific AI Agents by 2026, Up from Less Than 5% in 2025"
- **Publisher:** Gartner, Inc.
- **Publication date:** August 26, 2025
- **Primary URL:** https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases/2025-08-26-gartner-predicts-40-percent-of-enterprise-apps-will-feature-task-specific-ai-agents-by-2026-up-from-less-than-5-percent-in-2025
- **Exact stat:** 40% of enterprise apps will embed task-specific AI agents by end of 2026, rising from <5% in 2025.
- **Recommended inline citation:** (Gartner, Aug. 2025)

### Stat 4: Indeed Hiring Lab — GenAI Job Posting Growth (170%)

- **Report:** "AI at Work: Rise of the GenAI Consultant"
- **Publisher:** Indeed Hiring Lab
- **Publication date:** February 27, 2025
- **Primary URL:** https://www.hiringlab.org/2025/02/27/ai-at-work-rise-of-the-genai-consultant/
- **Exact stat:** Share of US job postings mentioning GenAI or related terms grew **170%** from January 2024 to January 2025.
- **Context:** In absolute terms this remained a small share of all postings (~0.1% → ~0.3% of all Indeed postings), but the growth rate is the relevant signal.
- **Recommended inline citation:** (Indeed Hiring Lab, Feb. 2025)
